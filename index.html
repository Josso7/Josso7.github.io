This is my story

My love for coding began on a calculator. While my high school peers paid attention in class and took notes I would sit huddled over my TI-84 calculator trying to figure out how to draw on the screen with code and create turn-based combat games. I couldn’t wait to get home and look up the TI-BASIC documentation and learn that last piece of code I couldn’t figure out while at school. That experience kindled something inside of me that would never go away.

I graduated high school and started working in the retail industry right away. That eventually led me to a 4-year career in sales. In those 4 years, I gained valuable lessons in communication, learned how to work fluidly with my team, and overcome workplace challenges. I was trained to think about the customer and focus on taking care of them. I learned how to tailor my company's products into a packaged solution for my customers in a way that provided value to their life. Problem solving became second nature to me. I decided I wanted something more challenging and wanted to follow my dream of being a software engineer. I was accepted into App Academy which is a 6 month, 1000+ hour fast paced software engineering bootcamp. I graduated in March 2022 and in the process, I learned Javascript, Python, React.js, Redux, Express, Flask, PostgreSQL, SQL Alchemy, HTML5, and CSS3. I also made several projects. ThePipe (thepipe-aa.herokuapp.com) is a YouTube clone. I used Cloudinary as a cloud storage host for users to seamlessly upload videos to the platform. I used modals to create well transitioned forms to create and edit videos.
